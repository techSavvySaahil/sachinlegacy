Sachin Tendulkar

Sachin Ramesh Tendulkar is a former Indian cricketer and captain. Made debut with Test match at the age of sixteen. He has many records on his name.

Some of his records are :
  • Most One-Day Internationals : 463
  • Most Man of the Match awards : 62
  • Most Man of the series awards : 15
  • Only one to accomplish rare ODI triple : 15000 runs(18426), 100 wickets(154) and 100(140) catches.
  • Most ODI runs : 18426 in 463 matches
  • Most Test runs : 15921
  • Most ODI centuries : 49
  • Most Test centuries : 51
  • Most runs in a single World Cup : 673 in 11 matches
  • Most centuries in World Cups : 6
  • First batsman to score an ODI double hundred : 200 not out
  • Fastest to reach 10000, 14000, 15000 runs in Tests
